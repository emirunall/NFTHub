About

NFTHub is a broad-based Web3 project built on the solana network that allows you to share and trade your memories on the Blockchain. It allows you to instantly share an event, plot or an article that tells about yourself. You can follow your friends from this platform, see their memories or situations and buy these memories in exchange for tokens. Equipped with a new technology, NFTHub allows you to create and share an NFT. A new NFT social media platform,for you.
